Stop Shooting: Save Our Youth

An anti gun violence campaign that consists of a documentary and a poster design to help raise awareness on gun violence, and the youth who are mostly affected in the neighborhood of Bedford Stuyvesant.

Melissa Martinez

Melissa Martinez is an artist interested in designing for social issues. She enjoys using design to raise awareness, such as, graphic design, mobile apps, websites, and film.

This is the poster design, which is based from the documentary.
