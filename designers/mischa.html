Octocom


Octocom is an immersive interactive installation that takes a critical view of the ways we communicate. In the world of wireless communication and cordless devices, the language of the physical body is dissolving into bytes and pixels. Octocom encourages participants to reclaim their physicality and engage in digital conversation that is enhanced and manipulated with the experience of the body.


Octocom enables participants to transmit messages by quantifying their biofeedback. Akin to FM radio waves, the users are encourage to synchronize their body impulses to achieve the same emotional state of being.




Mischa Abakumova


I make fun things for people to play with.

Mischa is a Brooklyn-based experience and interaction designer with background in architecture and art festival production. Her work is inspired by the symbiosis of organic form and technology. Mischa teaches technology to children at BlinkBlink and explores hard-to-reach corners of the world in spare time.
