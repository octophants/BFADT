Mood


The air is mysterious. It is invisible in our daily life. We need air, but unfortunately, air pollution is generally present, especially in the cities. The “Mood” will present pollution levels of cities, thereby representing urban 'moods' through the multiple color overlay setting. Using the real data of air quality transfer along with the visual aspects of “Mood” will allow spectators to watch the flowing air patterns of cities.


Angela Huitian Chen

Angela Huitian Chen is a New York City interactive designer, who experiments with UI/UX, Physical computing and Motion Graphic.





The color of “Mood” is based on the public daily air quality index. “Mood” represents the real-time data of AQI, PM2.5, PM10, O3, SO2, CO, HUMIDITY, TEMPERATURE, WIND.
