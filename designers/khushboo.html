Intertidal

Climate change has lead to rising sea levels all over the world. Intertidal, a Virtual Reality underwater exploration, gives the viewer a glimpse into the possible future of the Philippines, a country that is constantly affected by natural disasters, such as typhoons and floods, if it continues to sink by several centimetres per year.

Khushboo Lalwani

Khushboo Lalwani is a New York city based digital designer from the Philippines who does Graphic Design, Motion Graphics, Post Production, CG, Animation and more. She is also interested in interaction Design - mainly Virtual Reality and Interactive Storytelling. Her inspiration for her work comes from her favorite Pixar, Disney and DreamWorks animated movies.
